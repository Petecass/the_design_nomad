Life is too short to spend boxed up in one place

Instead I want to live, breathe and experience as many new places and cultures as I can.

My bucketlist is ever expanding, and I try everyday to keep up. No complaints though; my insatiable wanderlust has me running around the world getting up to various (but thoroughly enjoyable) mischiefs - all the while funneling the insights from all I see and do into running my UX & UI design studio, MELEWI with the help from my amazing team.

The world is my office, and it’s impossible not to be inspired when you’ve the freedom to find yourself the best vantage point.

My name is Mel. I’m 24, and was born in Singapore, but if I can be annoying for a sec - I’d like to think of myself as a citizen of the world.

My days are spent figuring out if I should read a business book, fire up Sketch (or Photoshop), read articles on design & UX, or work on startups ideas. Or sniff out the nearest artisanal cafe - I can’t grow a moustache, so this is how I hipster.
